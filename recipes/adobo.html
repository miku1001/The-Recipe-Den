Adobo

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly (or ribs), cut into chunks
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pack adobo mix (e.g., tamarind-based)
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced thinly
  • 6-8 string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2 long green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt or fish sauce (patis), to taste
